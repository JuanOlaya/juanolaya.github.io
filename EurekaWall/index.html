EurekaWall

Physical environments can have an impact on creativity, but, how to adjust a coworking space to inspire creativity?

EurekaWall is a toolkit to unlock the generation of novel ideas, it offers a set of 4 interactive experiences to enhance the incubation effect during creative processes.

The incubation effect is the result, of putting a problem aside during an incubation interval, and by returning to it, being able to rearrange the problem to find a solution.

MuseFlow

Undemanding and relaxing video game to unlock creativity promoting mind-wandering episodes during break times.


ParticleCanvas

Undemanding and relaxing video game to unlock creativity promoting mind-wandering episodes during break times.


Balconity

Digital balcony to expand the mind and inspire new ideas in coworking spaces.


PixelGrid

Interactive experience to facilitate positive moods, autonomy and personal meaningful.
